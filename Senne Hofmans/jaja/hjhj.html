Bio

Fleddy Melculy is a Belgian rock musician and songwriter known for his energetic live performances and powerful voice. He first gained fame as the lead singer of the band Fleddy Melculy, which became a household name in Belgium and the Netherlands in the late 2010s with hits like "De Kop Van De Kat" and "Hallelujah". In 2021, Melculy released his debut solo album "Het Grote Gelijk", which topped the charts in Belgium and received critical acclaim for its raw and emotional lyrics. In addition to his music career, Melculy is also known for his philanthropic work and involvement in social and political causes. He is currently on tour in Europe and North America, performing to sold-out crowds at some of the world's most iconic venues.

Music

"De Kop Van De Kat" ("The Head of the Cat"): This upbeat rock anthem became Fleddy Melculy's breakout hit in 2017. It features catchy guitar riffs and lyrics about standing up for oneself and not backing down in the face of adversity. "Hallelujah": This powerful ballad was released in 2018 and quickly became a fan favorite. It showcases Melculy's emotive vocals and tells the story of a person struggling with their faith and seeking salvation. "Zatte Vrienden" ("Drunk Friends"): This humorous and upbeat track was released in 2019 and became a popular party anthem. It celebrates the joys of friendship and good times with a catchy chorus and sing-along lyrics. "Ik Ben Een Maniak" ("I Am a Maniac"): This intense and energetic track was released in 2020 and showcases Melculy's raw and unfiltered vocal style. It deals with themes of mental health and self-destructive behavior. "Het Grote Gelijk" ("The Great Truth"): This title track from Melculy's debut solo album was released in 2021 and became a hit in Belgium. It features introspective and thought-provoking lyrics about society and the search for meaning in life.

Tour

"De Kop Van De Kat" Tour (2017-2018): This was Fleddy Melculy's first major tour, which took place in Belgium, the Netherlands, and Germany. It featured over 50 shows in some of the biggest venues in the region and was a massive success, selling out almost every show. "Hallelujah" Tour (2018-2019): This tour expanded on the success of the previous one, adding dates in France, Switzerland, and Austria to the schedule. It featured over 70 shows and once again, was a huge success, with many shows selling out within hours of going on sale. "Zatte Vrienden" Tour (2019-2020): This tour took Melculy to new heights, with shows in the United Kingdom, the United States, and Canada. It featured over 100 shows and was praised for its high-energy performances and memorable stage production. "Ik Ben Een Maniak" Tour (2021): This tour saw Melculy returning to his roots and focusing on smaller, more intimate shows. It featured over 30 shows in Belgium and the Netherlands and was praised for its raw and emotional performances. "Het Grote Gelijk" Tour (2022-2023): This is Melculy's current tour, which is taking him to even more countries around the world. It features over 100 shows and is expected to be another huge success.
